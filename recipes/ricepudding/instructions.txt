    STEP 1
    Rinse porridge rice in cold water and put them in a pot with 1 dl water.
    
    STEP 2
    Bring to the boil while stirring. 
    When the water has evaporated, pour 2 liters of milk into the porridge rice
    and stir well for a few minutes, while heating to just below boiling point.   
    
    STEP 3
    Turn down to low heat and stir further for a minute.
    Put the lid on the pan and let it stand on low heat.
    Stir in the pan approx. every 10 minutes until the rice is tender
    and the porridge is delicious.  
    
    STEP 4
    Add a little salt and serve the hot rice porridge with butter and cinnamon.
