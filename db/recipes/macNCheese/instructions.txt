SETP 1
Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit. In a 6-qt. stockpot, cook macaroni according to package directions, adding broccoli, cauliflower, 
carrots and celery during the last 6 minutes of cooking. Drain; transfer to a greased 13x9-in. baking dish.

STEP 2
Meanwhile, in a large saucepan, heat butter over medium-high heat; saute onion until tender. 
Stir in flour until blended. Gradually stir in milk; bring to a boil. 
Cook and stir until thickened, about 2 minutes; stir in cheese, mustard, salt and pepper.

STEP 3
Add to macaroni mixture, stirring to coat; sprinkle with paprika. Bake, uncovered, until heated through, 15-20 minutes.

NUTRITION
1 cup: 200 calories, 11g fat (6g saturated fat), 33mg cholesterol, 391mg sodium, 15g carbohydrate (3g sugars, 2g fiber), 10g protein.
