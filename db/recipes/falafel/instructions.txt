STEP 1
In a large bowl, cover chickpeas with water. Stir in 1/2 teaspoon baking soda. 
Cover and let stand overnight. Drain chickpeas; rinse and pat dry.

STEP 2
In a food processor, pulse cilantro and mint until finely chopped. 
Add chickpeas, garlic, salt, pepper if desired, coriander and chili powder. 
Pulse until blended and texture of coarse meal. Transfer to a large bowl. 
Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour.

STEP 3
Stir in sesame seeds and baking powder. Shape into sixteen 2-in. balls. 
In an electric skillet or a deep-fat fryer, heat oil to 375 degrees fahrenheit. 
Fry chickpea balls, a few at a time, until golden brown, about 2 minutes, turning occasionally. Drain on paper towels.

NUTRITION
4 pieces: 224 calories, 13g fat (1g saturated fat), 0 cholesterol, 760mg sodium, 32g carbohydrate (1g sugars, 16g fiber), 9g protein.
