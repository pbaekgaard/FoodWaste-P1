STEP 1
In a large bowl, combine the first 13 ingredients. Place chicken and sausage in a 6-qt. slow cooker. Pour tomato mixture over top.
Cook, covered, on low until chicken is tender, 7-9 hours.

STEP 2
Stir in shrimp. Cook, covered, until shrimp turn pink, 15-20 minutes longer. Serve with rice.

NUTRITION
1 cup jambalaya with 2/3 cup cooked rice: 387 calories, 10g fat (3g saturated fat), 164mg cholesterol, 674mg sodium, 37g carbohydrate (4g sugars, 4g fiber),
36g protein. Diabetic Exchanges: 3 lean meat, 2-1/2 starch.
