STEP 1
Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit. Toss together first 5 ingredients; place in a shallow greased 2-qt. baking dish.

STEP 2
Bake, covered, 30 minutes. Sprinkle with cheese; bake, uncovered, until vegetables are tender, about 10 minutes.

NUTRITION

5g carbohydrate (3g sugars, 1g fiber), 5g protein. Diabetic Exchanges: 1 medium-fat meat, 1 vegetable.
